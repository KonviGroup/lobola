LobolaTM brings efficiency and simplicity to cooking pap. Through automation, the human element is excused and time is spared for more important things.

LobolaTM is a next generation automated pap cooking appliance, designed for performance and elegance. The name originates from an african word that means bride price. It is so called, because it's the ultimate gift for a modern family. LobolaTM can be used at home, braai (barbeques), for mass cooking, food markets, etc.
