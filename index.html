Inspiration

The existence of a rice-cooker inspired LobolaTM. An appliance that one can simply insert ingredients into and leave it to do the rest, only this time for pap (South Africa's indigenous staple meal).

Having grown up eating pap, we wanted to develop something that improved the process of cooking it - especially since itâ€™s consumed daily by about 46% of South African households.

About Pap

Pap is inexpensive to make (due to the ingredients), tasty, and filling. However, preparing it is strenuous and requires skills. This makes the process both time consuming and difficult at the same time.

LobolaTM saves time and effort to make South Africa's most cherished staple. We designed an appliance that will be both effective and efficient while looking elegant.

feather
Modern Kitchen

Everything in the kitchen is becoming more efficient when it comes to preparing meals. Modern appliances have simplified cooking and made it more desirable, e.g. air fryers.

Another trend is that cooks prefer easy-to-make recipes, spending minimal time in the kitchen, and using as little electricity as possible. This is the reason books like The Lazy Makoti are popular. LobolaTM is currently what's missing to simplify pap cooking for the modern cook.
